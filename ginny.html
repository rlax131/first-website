GINNY WEASLEY


R Ginevra "Ginny" Weasley is a fictional character in J.K. Rowling's "Harry Potter" series. As the youngest child and only daughter of the Weasley family, Ginny grows from a shy girl into a brave and strong-willed individual. She first appears in "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone" and is sorted into Gryffindor House at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.


HOUSE: Gryffindor
FULL NAME: Ginevra Molly Weasley
BORN: 11 August 1981
FAMILY: Arthur Weasley, Molly Weasley etc.
GINNY'S PERSONALITY
