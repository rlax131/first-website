The primary mode of transport to Hogwarts is the Hogwarts Express that students take at the start of each school year. Students board the train from the also fictional and hidden Platform 9+3⁄4 at King's Cross station in London. The train arrives at Hogsmeade station near Hogwarts, sometime after nightfall.
