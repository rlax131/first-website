DRACO MALFOY


Draco Malfoy, born 5 June 1980, was a British pure-blood wizard, son of Lucius and Narcissa Malfoy. Raised with strong beliefs in blood purity, he disdained half-bloods and Muggle-borns. Draco attended Hogwarts from 1991 to 1998 and was immediately sorted into Slytherin. At Hogwarts, he befriended fellow Slytherins like Vincent Crabbe and Gregory Goyle, but also became a rival to Harry Potter.
