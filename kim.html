Professors in charge of the school

ALBUS DUMBLE DORE

Professor Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore was an English half-blood wizard. He was the Defence Against the Dark Arts Professor, later the Transfiguration Professor, and later the Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He was considered to have been the greatest wizard of modern times.

Minerva McGonagall

Professor Minerva McGonagall was a Scottish half-blood witch, the only daughter of Muggle Robert McGonagall. Minerva was Sorted into Gryffindor House, but it took the Sorting Hat few minutes to decide if she was Gryffindor or Ravenclaw, which made her a Hatstall. During her seventh year, she was appointed Head Girl.

OTHER PROFESSORS

SEVERUS SNAPE

Severus Snape was a key figure in the Wizarding Wars, serving in multiple roles at Hogwarts and as a member of both the Order of the Phoenix and the Death Eaters.

FILIUS FLITWICK

Filius Flitwick, a part-goblin wizard and former model student of Ravenclaw, returned to Hogwarts as the Charms Master and Head of Ravenclaw House after his graduation.

POMONA SPROUT

Pomona Sprout, a Hufflepuff alumna and Herbology expert, served as Head of Hufflepuff House and the Herbology department at Hogwarts.

RUBEUS HAGRID

Rubeus Hagrid, a half-giant wizard, was unjustly expelled from Hogwarts but remained as the school's gamekeeper, thanks to Albus Dumbledore's intervention.
